BACKGROUND
The Creditor has agreed to extend a business term loan of RM 150,000.00
to the Principal Debtor. As a condition of the loan facility, the
Guarantor executes this Deed of Guarantee and Indemnity in favour
of the Creditor.

1. GUARANTEE — JOINT AND SEVERAL LIABILITY
   The Guarantor hereby irrevocably and unconditionally guarantees to
   the Creditor the due and punctual payment of all sums now or
   hereafter owing by the Principal Debtor.

   The Guarantor accepts JOINT AND SEVERAL LIABILITY with the
   Principal Debtor. The Creditor may at its sole discretion pursue
   the Guarantor for the full outstanding amount without first making
   demand on or exhausting remedies against the Principal Debtor.

2. UNLIMITED LIABILITY
   This Guarantee covers the entire outstanding balance including:
   - Principal outstanding
   - All accrued and future interest
   - Penalty charges and late fees
   - Legal costs and solicitor fees (on full indemnity basis)
   - Any other charges the Creditor deems applicable
   There is NO cap or ceiling on the Guarantor's total liability
   under this Deed. Liability is unlimited in scope and amount.

3. CONTINUING GUARANTEE
   This Guarantee is a continuing guarantee and shall remain in full
   force and effect until all monies owed by the Principal Debtor to
   the Creditor are fully settled. There is NO automatic release clause.
   The Guarantor may NOT terminate this Guarantee by notice.

4. DIRECT LEGAL RECOVERY CLAUSE
   Without prejudice to any other rights, the Creditor may initiate
   civil proceedings directly against the Guarantor upon any event
   of default by the Principal Debtor, including:
   - Attachment of the Guarantor's personal bank accounts
   - Garnishment of the Guarantor's salary or business income
   - Seizure and sale of the Guarantor's personal property
   - Bankruptcy proceedings against the Guarantor
   No court order against the Principal Debtor is required before
   the Creditor exercises rights against the Guarantor.

5. DEMAND ON GUARANTOR
   A written demand by the Creditor shall be conclusive evidence that
   the amount stated therein is due and payable by the Guarantor.
   The Guarantor has no right to dispute the amount in the demand
   notice.

6. INDEMNITY
   The Guarantor further agrees to indemnify and keep indemnified
   the Creditor against all losses, costs, expenses and liabilities
   arising from any failure by the Principal Debtor to perform its
   obligations under the Loan Agreement.

7. WAIVER OF RIGHTS
   The Guarantor waives all rights of:
   - First requiring the Creditor to proceed against the Principal Debtor
   - Set-off or counter-claim against the Creditor
   - Any defence available to the Principal Debtor
   - Relief under any statute, rule or regulation

8. CHANGES TO PRINCIPAL AGREEMENT
   This Guarantee shall not be affected or discharged by any
   amendment, variation, waiver, or extension granted by the Creditor
   to the Principal Debtor, even without notice to the Guarantor.

9. GOVERNING LAW
   This Deed is governed by the laws of Malaysia.
